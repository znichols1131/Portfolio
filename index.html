I Like To Build Solutions

Zach Nichols

Software Developer from Indianapolis

I'm currently building a formal background in Software Development at Eleven Fifty Academy. After 3+ years as an MEP Engineer, I decided to chase my life-long passion of coding full-time. I'm no stranger to problem-solving and fast-paced team environments, and I'm excited to explore this ever-growing field.

As part of my current studies, I've used C#, ASP.NET, SQL, HTML, and CSS to create several console apps and APIs. My portfolio website and D&D Character Creator team project are just the latest examples, but I'm a perpetual learner that loves to tackle new coding languages and technologies for personal projects (iOS development, C/Robot C, Javascript).

My experiences as a Mechanical Engineer lend me a unique perspective when it comes to problem-solving and customer-oriented solutions. As an engineer in the MEP consulting industry, I collaborated with other engineering firms, contractors, and Fortune 500 clients to deliver comprehensive building mechanical systems in the industrial, pharmaceutical, and corporate office sectors. I headed several pipe stress analyses for steam distribution systems, provided on-site construction administration for turn-key operation, and secured repeat business through practical consultation and customer service.

I plan to bring this same attention to detail, client interactions, and collaborative teamwork to the software development industry. I'm eager to get my foot in the door and start developing full-time!
